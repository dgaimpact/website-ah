FAQ

Common Questions

What is AI visibility and why does it matter?
Over 51% of web traffic is now automated — AI systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini are constantly evaluating your online presence to determine if you should be recommended to people asking for professionals in your area. If you're not optimized for AI comprehension, you're invisible to this growing channel.
Do you guarantee AI will recommend me?
No. AI platforms are third-party systems outside our control. What we do guarantee is that your online presence will be systematically optimized with the authority signals AI systems use to evaluate professionals. We control the infrastructure — the outcomes depend on how AI platforms process those signals.
What does "geographic exclusivity" mean?
Ontario is divided into 78 defined market territories. Each territory has limited slots per profession. Once you claim your market for your profession, competitors in that same profession and market cannot subscribe. This protects your investment and creates sustainable competitive advantage.
How is this different from SEO or social media marketing?
Traditional SEO optimizes for Google's search algorithm. Social media marketing targets human followers. Authority Hub™ optimizes for AI recommendation engines — a fundamentally different audience that requires structured, declarative content rather than persuasive marketing copy.
How much time does this take from me?
After initial onboarding (Voice Blueprint interview + Information Intake), the system runs on autopilot. Monthly audits, content generation, and multi-platform publishing all happen automatically. You'll spend about 15 minutes per month reviewing your dashboard.
What's the difference between Solo and Playbook Hub?
Solo gives you the full AI visibility platform for yourself. Playbook Hub adds referral network capabilities — connect up to 5 complementary professionals (your mortgage broker, your lawyer, your accountant) and cross-reference each other in AI content. Think of it as a BNI-style referral group, but for AI visibility.
