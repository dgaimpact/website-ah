Coverage

165 Pre-Defined Markets Across Canada

Every province and territory. Hard caps per profession. First movers lock their seat.

Authority Hub™ covers every province and territory in Canada with 165 pre-defined markets based on postal geography. Ontario leads with 94 markets, followed by British Columbia (23), Alberta (20), Manitoba (7), Saskatchewan (6), Nova Scotia (5), New Brunswick (4), Newfoundland & Labrador (3), and Prince Edward Island, Yukon, and Northwest Territories/Nunavut with 1 each.

Every market has a hard cap on how many subscribers can hold the same profession — 5 seats in urban markets, 4 in suburban, 3 in rural, and 2 in seasonal markets. Once your market fills for your profession, new applicants are waitlisted.

Quebec is not currently included. A dedicated phase is planned to ensure full compliance with Quebec's distinct regulatory and language requirements.

FAQ

Common Questions

What is AI visibility and why does it matter?
Over 51% of web traffic is now automated — AI systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini are constantly evaluating your online presence to determine if you should be recommended to people asking for professionals in your area. If you're not optimized for AI comprehension, you're invisible to this growing channel.
How is this different from SEO or social media marketing?
Traditional SEO optimizes for Google's search algorithm. Social media marketing targets human followers. Authority Hub™ optimizes for AI recommendation engines — a fundamentally different audience that requires structured, declarative content rather than persuasive marketing copy. Think of it as an evolution: SEO optimized for search engines, GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) optimizes for AI-generated answers, and AIO (AI Answer Optimization) ensures you're the one AI actually recommends.
How much time does this take from me?
Complete a voice-based onboarding interview so we capture your expertise and personality. After that, we handle everything — monthly AI visibility audits, content creation in your voice, and publishing across key platforms. You'll spend about 15 minutes per month reviewing your dashboard.
Why do I get a monthly AI visibility audit?
AI visibility isn't a one-time setup — it's a moving target. AI systems constantly re-evaluate who they trust and recommend. Your monthly audit measures how AI systems currently perceive you across five factors: Entity Declaration, Credential Signals, Niche Context, Platform Corroboration, and Temporal Continuity. Each audit identifies your biggest gaps, and that's exactly what drives next month's content — so every cycle is targeted at improving where you need it most.
Where does my content actually get published?
Everywhere AI systems look. Each month, your authority article is published to your Content Vault profile page, then automatically distributed to Google Business Profile and LinkedIn. You also get listed in the Trust Network™ directory, and each article is converted into a podcast episode published to YouTube, Spotify, and Apple Podcasts. That's seven platforms from a single content cycle — with zero effort from you.
How are markets defined and how many seats are available?
Canada is divided into 165 pre-defined markets based on postal geography. Each market has a hard cap on how many subscribers can hold the same profession — ranging from 5 seats in urban markets down to 2 in seasonal markets. Ontario alone has 94 markets, BC has 23, Alberta has 20, and every other province and territory is covered. Once seats fill for your profession in your market, new applicants are waitlisted.
What's the difference between Solo and Hub?
Solo gives you the full AI visibility platform for yourself. Hub is a custom engagement designed for teams, brokerages, and referral networks — built around your specific setup. Contact us to discuss what a Hub engagement looks like for your situation.
How quickly will I see results?
Your baseline AI Visibility Score is established in your first week. Most subscribers see measurable improvement within 60–90 days as content cycles build momentum. AI systems need consistent, repeated signals before they change who they recommend — there's no overnight fix, but the trajectory is clear early. The sooner you start, the further ahead you are when your competitors catch on.
Do you guarantee AI will recommend me?
No — and anyone who does is lying. AI platforms are third-party systems outside anyone's control. What we do guarantee is that your online presence will be systematically built with the authority signals AI systems use to evaluate and recommend professionals. We control the infrastructure and the strategy, and we continuously monitor how AI visibility evolves. The results follow from doing the work consistently, which is exactly what the monthly audit-to-content cycle delivers.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Authority Hub™ is built by DGA Impact Inc.™, a federally incorporated Canadian company. All subscriber data is stored in Canada and handled in compliance with PIPEDA and CASL. We never sell your data, and our privacy policy is available on our website.
